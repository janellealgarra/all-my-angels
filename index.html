ELIZA MARIE

" Wait you pause and then you play I’ll be your playlist for today ..."



Eliza Marie is an indie pop/lo-fi artist from the Philippines who writes about feelings, cats, and angels among other things.

The Filipino-American singer was recently signed into the record label company Off-Shore Music and released her debut single "I Wonder" under the label. She draws her influence from artists such as, Lennon Stella, Magie Rogers, and Clairo that amplify her girly-DIY sound. Her music tells stories of what it feels like to be a young woman experiencing growth and change in an ever-evolving world.
